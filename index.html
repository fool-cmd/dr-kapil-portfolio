The Journey

From the Operating Table
to the Director's Chair

Dr. Kapil Rijal is a Nepal-born filmmaker, producer, entrepreneur, and former dental surgeon based in the United Kingdom.

Dr. Kapil Rijal is a highly acclaimed Nepali filmmaker, visionary producer, and dedicated entrepreneur who has spent decades living and working in the United Kingdom. Originally establishing himself as a highly successful dental surgeon and implantologist, Dr. Rijal ultimately chose to follow his true calling, making a bold transition to pursue filmmaking as his full-time profession.

His deep-rooted passion for cinema is an inherited legacy. He is the son of the legendary Nepali gynecologist, esteemed lyricist, and prolific filmmaker Dr. Bhola Rijal, whose monumental contributions have profoundly shaped both Nepali cinema and literature. Carrying this artistic torch forward, Kapil established BR Production and KR Production in London.

"Cinema has the power to inspire change and preserve our stories."

— Dr. Kapil Rijal

Dr. Rijal's directorial portfolio includes critically acclaimed works such as Harry Ki Pyari, Gauthali, and Karma. Through his lens, he consistently strives to tell authentic, socially resonant stories that not only celebrate cultural heritage but also inspire tangible social transformation.

Today, alongside his ambitious filmmaking endeavors, he serves as an influential entrepreneur and holds the prestigious position of Chairman of the Britain Nepal Chamber of Commerce (BNCC). Furthermore, remaining true to his medical roots, he regularly organizes free medical and dental camps in rural regions of Nepal.
